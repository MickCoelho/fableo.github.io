Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Fableo ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. By using Fableo, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

From Parents (Account Holders)

  • Contact Information: Email address and name provided during sign-up (via Email, Google, or Apple Sign-In).
  • Payment Information: Transaction history for subscriptions or credits (processed securely via Apple App Store). We do not store credit card details.

From Child Profiles (Created by Parents)

Parents voluntarily provide the following information to generate personalized stories:

  • Profile Details: First name (or nickname), age, gender, and interests.
  • Appearance: Digital avatar preferences (e.g., hair color, eye color) or an optional photo. Note: Photos are processed locally on your device and used solely for generating avatar references; they are not used to train public AI models.
  • Story Inputs: Voice recordings and text prompts used to create stories.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: We collect only anonymous usage data (e.g., crash reports, features accessed) to help us improve the app. We do not track individual users across other apps or websites.
  • Device Information: Device model and operating system version for troubleshooting.

Advertising & Tracking

We do NOT use third-party advertising. Fableo is ad-free. We do not use third-party analytics software that collects personally identifiable information (PII) from children. Any analytics used are strictly for internal app performance and do not track users for behavioral advertising purposes.

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide the Service: Generating personalized stories and illustrations based on your inputs.
  • To Maintain Safety: filtering text and audio inputs to prevent inappropriate content generation.
  • To Manage Accounts: syncing family data across devices securely.
  • To Improve Fableo: analyzing usage patterns to fix bugs and enhance features.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We partner with trusted third-party services to power Fableo. These partners adhere to strict data security standards:

  • Google Firebase: For secure cloud storage, authentication, and database services.
  • Google Gemini API: For generating story text and creative content.
  • Cartesia / ElevenLabs: For text-to-speech audio narration.
  • Apple iCloud / Keychain: For secure on-device storage of credentials.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your child's personal data for advertising purposes.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. Specifically:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active. You may delete your account at any time in the app settings.
  • Story Content: Retained until you choose to delete specific stories or your entire account.
  • Temporary Processing Data: Voice recordings used for transcription are processed and immediately discarded or deleted after the story is generated, unless you choose to save them (feature dependent).

6. Parental Rights & Control

As a parent or guardian, you have full control over your family's data:

  • Access: You can view all child profiles and created stories within the app.
  • Edit: You can update profile information at any time.
  • Delete: You can delete child profiles or specific stories directly within the app. To request full account deletion and removal of all associated data, please contact us or use the "Delete Account" feature in Settings.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest.
  • Family-based access controls (only members of your family group can see your data).
  • Biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID) for accessing sensitive settings (Parent Zone).

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K)

Fableo is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and GDPR-K.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 without verified parental consent (obtained via the Apple App Store purchase/download process and account creation).
  • If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child without parental verification, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
