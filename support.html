Frequently Asked Questions

How do credits work?

Fableo uses credits to generate personalized AI stories. Each new story costs a specific number of credits (usually 1). You can purchase more credits in the Settings menu.

How do I delete my account?

We value your privacy. You can delete your account and all associated data (including child profiles and stories) directly in the app. Go to Settings > Danger Zone > Reset Data / Delete Account.

My story didn't generate correctly. What can I do?

AI can sometimes be unpredictable. If a story fails to generate but credits were deducted, please contact us at support@fableo.app with your account email, and we will help you.
